# Judging Strategy

The project is designed to score on sponsor integration depth and product clarity.

- CMC is used as the market intelligence and regime layer, not just a price feed.
- Trust Wallet is the custody and execution boundary, with quote-only and token-risk paths modeled before signing.
- BNBAgent SDK gives the agent a public service identity and status surface.
- BNB Chain is the venue focus, with BSC DEX route modeling and testnet identity defaults.
- Aave V3 makes the USP concrete: temporary liquidity for atomic pool-balancing simulations.

The repo favors honesty over fake claims. Mocked pieces are labeled, real-mode setup is documented, and live execution remains disabled.
